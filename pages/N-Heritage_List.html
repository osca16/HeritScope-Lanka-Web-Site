Ella - Demodara

Nested in the heart of Sri Lanka's hill country, Ella is a picturesque town that captivates visitors with its serene beauty, lush greenery, and rich history. Surrounded by rolling tea plantations, misty mountains, and cascading waterfalls, Ella offers a perfect getaway for nature lovers, adventurers, and history enthusiasts alike.

Ravana Falls

Ravana Falls is a popular waterfall in Sri Lanka, located near Ella. It is known for its picturesque beauty and is a must-visit for tourists exploring the region.

Bambarakanda Falls

Bambarakanda Falls is the tallest waterfall in Sri Lanka, plunging from a height of 263 meters. It is surrounded by lush greenery and offers a breathtaking sight for visitors.

Diyaluma Falls

Diyaluma Falls is another stunning waterfall near Ella, known for its multiple cascades and natural pools. It is a popular spot for hiking and photography.
