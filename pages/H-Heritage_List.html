Sigiriya

Sigiriya, also known as the Lion Rock, is an ancient rock fortress in Sri Lanka. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is famous for its stunning frescoes, water gardens, and breathtaking views from the summit.

Isurumuniya

Isurumuniya is a famous Buddhist temple in Anuradhapura, known for its beautiful stone carvings, including the famous 'Lovers' sculpture, depicting ancient Sri Lankan artistry.

Yapahuwa

Yapahuwa is an ancient rock fortress that once served as the capital of Sri Lanka. It is known for its majestic staircase leading to the ruins of a royal palace.
