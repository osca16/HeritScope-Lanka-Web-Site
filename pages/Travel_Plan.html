  • Day 1 - Sigiriya & Dambulla

    Start your journey with the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Later, visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, the largest cave temple complex in Sri Lanka.

  • Day 2 - Kandy & Temple of the Tooth

    Explore the cultural city of Kandy, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic. Visit the Royal Botanical Gardens in Peradeniya and enjoy a scenic lake walk.

  • Day 4 - Adventure in Kitulgala

    Experience adventure sports in Kitulgala, including white-water rafting and exploring the prehistoric Belilena Cave, where ancient human remains were discovered.

  • Day 5 - Galle Fort & Unawatuna Beach

    End your trip with a visit to the Galle Dutch Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Relax at Unawatuna Beach or visit the iconic stilt fishermen in Weligama.
