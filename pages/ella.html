Ella - Demodara

Nested in the heart of Sri Lanka's hill country, Ella is a picturesque town that captivates visitors with its serene beauty, lush greenery, and rich history. Surrounded by rolling tea plantations, misty mountains, and cascading waterfalls, Ella offers a perfect getaway for nature lovers, adventurers, and history enthusiasts alike.

Renowned for its breathtaking viewpoints, Ella features iconic attractions like Ella Rock, Little Adam's Peak, and the Nine Arches Bridge, a marvel of colonial engineering. The area is steeped in folklore, with ties to the legendary tales of King Ravana from the Ramayana, adding a mystical charm to its allure.

Whether you're hiking through verdant trails, exploring ancient caves like Ravana Ella Cave, or simply savoring the tranquil ambiance with a warm cup of local tea, Ella promises an unforgettable experience. It's a haven where time slows down, and every moment feels like a journey through paradise.
